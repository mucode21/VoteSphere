Global Board 2024

The annual selection of the Global Board representatives. Ensure your voice is heard in shaping the future of the decentralized ledger.

Overview & Rules

The Global Board consists of three pivotal roles designed to guide protocol development and treasury management. Voting is open to all token holders who meet the staking requirements outlined below.

Eligibility Criteria

  • Minimum stake of 500 VTS tokens for 30 consecutive days prior to snapshot.
  • No active slashing penalties on the registered wallet.
  • One wallet, one vote principle applies.
